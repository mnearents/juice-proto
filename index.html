The number of minutes to wait before fulfilling an order. We recommend setting this to at least 1 minute to allow other apps — such as shipping label generators or order taggers — to do their job. In some cases, 10 minutes or more is needed.

Dunning schedule
When a payment fails, we retry the charge on the schedule below before marking the subscription as Failed. The subscription will remain Active until all retries are exhausted. You can add up to 7 retry attempts.
Soft failures (e.g. insufficient funds, do not honor) are retried without notifying the customer. The customer is only notified if the charge fails again on the next retry attempt. Hard failures (e.g. card lost or stolen, invalid account) always notify immediately.
3DS authentication timeout
When a payment requires 3D Secure authentication, customers have a limited window to complete it before the charge is abandoned and retried. Maximum 30 days.
